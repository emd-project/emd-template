## Introduction

This article is a **model**. It is a reference for writing: it shows the blog layout
(sticky sidebar + table of contents) and the editorial components available. The golden
rule: well-structured headings produce a good table of contents and good SEO automatically.

## How to read this page

### The table of contents (sidebar)

On the right (on large screens), the **sticky sidebar** shows the table of contents. It is
built automatically from the `##` (H2) and `###` (H3) headings. Nothing to do manually:
just write real headings, in order.

### The “Key takeaways” box

At the top of the article, the “Key takeaways” box reuses the `aiSummary` frontmatter field
(3 to 5 bullets). It is what readers — and search engines — read first.
